Purpose

History and music have influenced one another for ages. The Harlem Renaissance was a catalyst for different creative and political movements. The intersection of history and music and the impact on popular cultural today continues to influence ours lens on identity and culture.



Harlem Renaissance

Harlem Renaissance served as a golden age of artists, musicians, writers, and others aimed at redefining black experience and identity. It was a cultural awakening that would have lasting influences on the whole world. For the first time black artists would become part of the mainstream with the mass production of music and writing. This allowed blacks to redefine their identities and explore with experiences.
